Gabrielle grew up on the north shore of Massachussetts, the youngest of three sisters. She graduated from Simmons College in 2013, majoring in English with Communications and Fine Art minors.


She has worked for the Alzheimer's Association MA/NH Chapter as a PR assistant and completed an internship with Boston Children's Hospital creating an educational film project for the Boston Center for Endometriosis.


Currently, Fiorenza works as the office manager of The Armenian Review, a small peer-reviewed academic journal located in Watertown, MA. She is also the office production assistant at Sarah Colt Productions, finishing work on the biographical documentary Walt Disney, which premiers fall 2015.
